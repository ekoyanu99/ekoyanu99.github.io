About Me

Hello world !!! I am Eko Yanuarso Budi, you can call me Eko or Yanu. I was born in Pemalang January 5, 2002. Pemalang is a district located in Central Java which is famous for producing pineapple fruit. My hobby was playing football. From junior high to early high school, I attended the Garuda Perkasa football school in Jatidiri Field. I am quite active in junior and senior high school organizational activities, including OSIS and PMR. In the organization, leadership and solidarity are trained, until a sense of kinship arises between administrators.

When my high school students became interested in cryptocurrency, it started with the Facebook group Pemburu Bitcoin Indonesia. I got to know someone and started asking questions about 2017. After that I met someone who invited me to invest through ico, helped promote a project in collaboration with the team I was following. In 2019 ico started to dim and was replaced with ieo at that time ieo Binance. Until now, I still follow events about cryptocurrency including the Harmony Angel programs which is still on pause and the Equibrillium Ambassador programs.
